# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
tickflow community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting comments, and personal or
political attacks.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the
maintainer via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
